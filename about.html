About CoSwap

CoSwap is a community-driven platform that helps people convert unused coupons into real value.

Every year, thousands of coupons go unused and expire. CoSwap gives them a second life by connecting buyers and sellers in a simple, transparent way.

Our goal is to create a safe, easy and fair marketplace where trust is built through ratings, reviews and responsible transactions.

Whether you want to sell unused coupons or discover great deals, CoSwap makes it easy.

Simple. Secure. Smart.
