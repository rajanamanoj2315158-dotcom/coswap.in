How CoSwap Works

1. List Your Coupon

Have an unused coupon? Upload the coupon details, expiry date and set your price.

2. Buyer Sends Request

Interested buyers send a request. You have 30 minutes to accept or ignore.

3. Mutual Confirmation

Both buyer and seller confirm the deal. After confirmation, chat opens.

4. Secure Chat

Chat stays active for 24 hours so both parties can complete the exchange.

5. Transaction Complete

Once completed, ratings can be given to build trust in the community.

6. Earn & Buy Again

Sell unused coupons, earn money and discover great deals from others.
